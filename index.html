Cyrstal Collector


You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the score to match. The value of each cyrstal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
